Welcome to the Crypto-Portfolio App

This single-page application (SPA) allows you to manage and monitor your cryptocurrency portfolio with ease. Whether you want to track your token balances, view historical data, or transfer tokens, this app provides all the tools you need.

Wallet Connection

Connect your MetaMask wallet to access and manage your tokens. This secure integration ensures that your assets remain safe while providing you with real-time data.

Watch List

Add your favorite tokens to a personalized watch list. Stay updated with current balances and keep an eye on market trends.

Historical Data

View historical token balances to analyze past performance. Use the date picker to select specific time periods and gain insights into your investments.
